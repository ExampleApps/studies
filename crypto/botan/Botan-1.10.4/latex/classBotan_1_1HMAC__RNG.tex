\subsection{Detailed Description}
\hyperlink{classBotan_1_1HMAC__RNG}{H\-M\-A\-C\-\_\-\-R\-N\-G} -\/ based on the design described in \char`\"{}\-On Extract-\/then-\/\-Expand
\-Key Derivation Functions and an H\-M\-A\-C-\/based K\-D\-F\char`\"{} by Hugo Krawczyk (henceforce, 'E-\/t-\/\-E')

However it actually can be parameterized with any two M\-A\-C functions, not restricted to \hyperlink{classBotan_1_1HMAC}{H\-M\-A\-C} (this variation is also described in Krawczyk's paper), for instance one could use \hyperlink{classBotan_1_1HMAC}{H\-M\-A\-C}(S\-H\-A-\/512) as the extractor and \hyperlink{classBotan_1_1CMAC}{C\-M\-A\-C}(A\-E\-S-\/256) as the P\-R\-F.
