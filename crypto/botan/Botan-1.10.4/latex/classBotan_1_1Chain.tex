\subsection{Detailed Description}
This class represents \hyperlink{classBotan_1_1Filter}{Filter} chains. A \hyperlink{classBotan_1_1Filter}{Filter} chain is an ordered concatenation of Filters, the input to a \hyperlink{classBotan_1_1Chain}{Chain} sequentially passes through all the Filters contained in the \hyperlink{classBotan_1_1Chain}{Chain}.
